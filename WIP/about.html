Designer. Family Man. Whatever.

I have been working as a Graphic Designer for the last 7 years, both in freelance and as an in-house guy at several places. I'm also real experienced at UI/UX, web design, and just over all I've got my stuff together. I can use Maya, Blender, the entire Adobe Suite of products, Sublime Text, and can Google things pretty well. I'm an award winning children's book illustrator, and choreographer. No, really.

Design started as a hobby and pretty quickly turned into work. Photography, music, video editing, website building, fine art, furniture design; I used to do it for fun, and now I do it for fun and money.

I currently work as a Graphic Designer for Dixie State University, and manage all of the Public Relations for the Student Association there, in addition to my normal freelance work. I am always interested in providing design services for anyone who needs it, whether it be the largest corporation, the smallest mom-and-pop shop, or non-profit organization trying to be heard. Head over to my contact page to get in touch with me.

I married a wonderful woman a little over three years ago, had a daughter one year ago, and in my spare time, you can find me playing guitar, spending time with my family, or rewatching every glorious second of Breaking Bad.
