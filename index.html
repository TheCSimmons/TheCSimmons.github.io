ABOUT

Hello! My name is Colton Simmons Campbell, and I make things.

I've spent a lot of time doing a lot of things over the last ten or so years, but the hat I've worn most often is the one that says "Graphic Designer" in a really cool font with a simple yet tasteful icon. It came with the beard and glasses.

My expertise is in brand development and messaging. I've done everything from logo to web to print to clothing design, copywriting, content strategy, adwords, music composition, photography, and I've also tried my hand at carpentry. Yeah. It's been a busy ten years.

Anyway, here is what you need to know: I am here to help you. Whatever you need, we can work together to make something amazing. Let's chat.

Feel free to shoot me an email, or hit me up through any form of social media you see fit.
