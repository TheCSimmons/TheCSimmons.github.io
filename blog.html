Kimball Hafen Maternity Photos

APRIL ELEVENTH, TWENTY FIFTEEN

I had the opportunity to flex my photography muscles this weekend (something I don't get to do as often as I would like). It reminded me how much I enjoy trying to capture candid moments, and why I picked up a camera in the first place. The weather was great, the light was good, and I think the pictures came out fantastically.

I bought a new lens a few weeks ago, and hadn't really had a chance to try it out. I was super stoked when this opportunity came up, and let me take it for a spin. It was wonderful! I love that thing!

One thing that I really like about photography, is that you really just have one chance to get THAT picture. Sure, you can blast a dozen picture within a second, but getting THE picture is hard work, and requires timing that just isn't present in my normal day to day design.

When you design something, you usually make revision after revision after revision until its perfect. Not so much with photography. I guess what I am trying to say is that design feels more like a play you've rehearsed for weeks, and photography is more like improv on the fly. Both are great, and not everyone can do both, but I sure enjoy the attempt.

Did you know that I have been in THREE operas in my lifetime? Crazy. I'll save that for another blog post.

New "Promotional" Video

APRIL FOURTH, TWENTY FIFTEEN

Somehow I ended up with some spare time this week. I know! I'm not sure how it happened either, but it did. So, after watching a bunch of Aaron Draplin videos about design, I decided to make one for myself, because why not?

I know it ain't much, but I like it a lot. That might not say a lot because it is coming from me, but whatever. I think I'll embed it on my website, instead of the super lame "under construction" banner I have up right now. I might leave it there after the site is up. I am that proud of it.

One of the hardest things about being a freelance graphic designer is not only getting your name heard, but getting it remembered. This video, I guess, is my attempt at being remembered. I might just throw this out into the world wild web and see what happens.

Yep, lets do that.

New Site, New Blog

MARCH 28, TWENTY FIFTEEN

I bought my first domain name today. That was weird. But sh!t, it was 99 cents!

For a while I've known that I would need to build a website for myself. I have tried websites like wordpress, and square space, and behance, and blah and blah, and whatever. I don't know if its the designer in me, or some deep dark supressed control freak deep within my soul that just can't do it with those. I have to know that I can customize how my website looks, HOWEVER I CHOOSE. Maybe I'm just picky. Maybe I'm a control freak. Let's hope for the former.

Anyway, long story slightly less long, I decided to teach myself how to build websites. Thanks to my good friend David, two books by Jon Duckett, and a thirst for knowledge, I was able to scrape togetherthis bad boy you see before you.

So, this is where my blog will be. Check it at your convenience.

Welcome aboard.
